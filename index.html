Rules
You have a bunch of cards. Each card contains an image. Of each image there are exactly two cards. The cards are placed in a random order. You get to turn them around. If you turned around two cards with the same image, you have a pair and your pair wil stay visible. If they don't match, the cards will turn back around. Find all the pairs so fast as possible!
